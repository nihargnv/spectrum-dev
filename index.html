About TechForge

We are a passionate community of tech enthusiasts, innovators, and problem-solvers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of technology. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where students can learn, collaborate, and build the future through hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies.

From beginner-friendly workshops to advanced hackathons, we foster growth, creativity, and technical excellence in every member of our community.
